Our Story

I rescued Ube from a trailer park when he was two months old. He was worms, flea, and tick infested, and if he didn't get treatment when he did he could've passed away. Ube is my best friend, confidant, protector, and favorite thing in the world. His name comes from a Filipino purple yam (I am Filipino) to honor apart of my heritage, as well as note my favorite color is purple!

Breed

Pitbull, Staffordshire Terrier, Chow Chow, and German Shepherd

Weight

About Ube's Breeds

All of Ube's breeds have a bad reputation and stigma about being an "aggressive" dog breed. In reality, they are the sweetest and most loving dogs, and it all comes back to the dog's owner.
